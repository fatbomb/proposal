To My Dear Joyee

Hey Joyee,

This year is going to be end. It's a quite long time since we met. This is not one of my best years of my life. But at least I met you. Tomar oi hashimukher ami khub lovi. Tomar mukhvar kora mukh amar dekhte valo lage na. Jani amader majhe jhogra hoy tomar mon shob somoy rakhte pari na but ami tomake chara thakte parbo na. Kemon jeno tomake chara amar din cholei na tmi rag kore thakle amar din tao jeno valo kate na. I want to see your smiling face always. I want to hold your hand until we become dead. Amader majhe shoto jhamela jhograr majheo ami tomar sathe thakte chai. Amr vul gulate rego but amr theke dure jeo na. Thakbe ki amar sathe sarata jibon?
